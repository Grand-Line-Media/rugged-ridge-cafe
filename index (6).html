Our Story
Where the Mountains Meet Your Morning

Tucked on Monument Road near the base of Colorado's rugged canyon country, we're a locally owned neighborhood spot serving breakfast, lunch, and some of the finest organic coffee you'll find in the valley. Whether you're heading into the backcountry or settling in with a friend, we've got a seat for you.

We keep things simple: real ingredients, warm service, and a cozy space that feels like home. Vegetarian and gluten-free options always available.
